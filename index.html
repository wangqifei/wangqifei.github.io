I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at EECS, UC Berkeley, Teleimmersion lab, working with Prof. Ruzena Bajcsy and Dr. Gregorij Kurillo .

I was an associate researcher at Microsoft Research, Beijing, from January 2013 to July 2014. I received the B.E. degree in information and computing science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Beijing, China, in 2007, and the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, in 2012.

I have published over 13 technical journal and conference papers. My research interests include image/video processing, video compression, multimedia streaming, computer vision and machine learning.
